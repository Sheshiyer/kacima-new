Cookie Policy

Last updated: July 2025

Kācima's website is deliberately minimal. We use no advertising trackers, no social media pixels, and no data brokers. The cookies we use exist only to make the site function and to understand traffic in aggregate.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites place on your device. They help a website remember your preferences, understand how visitors use the site, and generally improve the browsing experience. Most browsers accept cookies by default, but you can change your settings at any time.

How we use cookies

We use a minimal set of cookies — no advertising, no cross-site tracking. Here is what we use and why:

Essential cookies

These cookies are required for the site to function. They help with things like security and form submissions. You cannot opt out of essential cookies while using the site.

Analytics cookies

We may use a privacy-respecting analytics tool (such as Plausible or a similar cookieless alternative) to understand approximate visitor numbers and how pages are browsed. This data is aggregated and anonymous — we cannot identify individual visitors. No third-party advertising platforms receive this data.

Preference cookies

If the site remembers a setting you have applied (such as a language or display preference), a small preference cookie may store that choice locally in your browser. These cookies are not shared with third parties.

What we do not use

We do not use:

  • Advertising or retargeting cookies
  • Social media tracking pixels (Facebook, Instagram, Google Ads, etc.)
  • Third-party cookies from data brokers
  • Any cookie that profiles you for commercial purposes

Third-party content

Our site may embed content from third parties — such as a Google Maps embed for directions. These services may set their own cookies according to their own policies. We recommend reviewing Google's privacy policy if this concerns you. We choose third-party embeds thoughtfully and limit them where possible.

Managing cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Here are guides for the most common browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Please note that disabling all cookies may affect the functionality of our contact form or other interactive features.

Changes to this policy

If we begin using new types of cookies, we will update this page. The date at the top reflects the latest revision.

Questions

If you have any questions about how we use cookies, please write to contractkacima@gmail.com.
