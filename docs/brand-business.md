# Kācima — Brand & Business Document

## Brand Positioning

### Core Positioning
**Kācima is a private farm retreat near Bangalore for deep rest, mindful movement, nature immersion and intimate transformational programs.**

### Positioning Statement
"A private nature retreat near Bangalore for rest, reflection, slow movement, healing and meaningful community."

### Brand Narrative
Kācima is an intimate, high-quality farm retreat centre on Kanakapura Road, Bangalore. The property is surrounded by dense greenery and countryside, with accommodation for up to 10 guests across 5 bedrooms.

The positioning should move beyond a regular farm stay or weekend villa and establish Kācima as a retreat-ready destination for:

- Yoga and meditation retreats
- Silent retreats and digital detox programs
- Breathwork and sound healing retreats
- Creative, art, music and self-expression retreats
- Founder, leadership and team reset programs
- Women's circles and community gatherings
- Nature, grounding and slow-living experiences
- International facilitator-led India retreats
- Small private group getaways with wellness add-ons

### Guardrails
To maintain a true healing environment:
- No alcohol
- No loud music
- Curated, farm-to-table vegetarian/vegan menu

### Unique Selling Points
- Private and intimate venue for up to 10 residential guests
- 5-bedroom accommodation: 3 villa bedrooms + 2 cottage rooms
- Farm and countryside setting close to Bangalore — easily accessible
- Dense greenery and nature-led environment
- Pool-facing rooms and villa-style accommodation
- Wellness experiences: massage, yoga, meditation, grounding, sound healing, nature walks
- Flexible use for hosted retreats, private buyouts, day retreats and community festivals
- Potential as a retreat hub for local practitioners and international facilitators
- 5-minute walk from Art of Living International Centre

### Brand Language
For the initial collaboration phase:
- **Naming**: "Kācima × People Ocean Community"
- Deep co-branding ("Kācima by People Ocean") to be explored only if partnership evolves into deeper operating/programming/brand partnership

## People Ocean Partnership

### Partnership Tiers

| Tier | Level | Description |
|------|-------|-------------|
| Tier 1 | Venue Partner | Kācima operates independently; People Ocean acts as booking channel. Standard commission per booking. No co-branding. |
| Tier 2 | Exclusive Curator | People Ocean manages entire retreat booking calendar and vetting. Jointly created schedules. Shared revenue model. |
| Tier 3 | "Kācima by People Ocean" | Deep co-branding, operational integration. People Ocean drives ethos, programming, global marketing. Shared margins. |

### Partnership Responsibilities

**Kācima provides**:
- Physical asset (luxury 5-bedroom farm sanctuary)
- On-ground property management
- Housekeeping
- Local vendor logistics
- Primary hospitality infrastructure

**People Ocean Community provides**:
- Brand identity
- Wellness programming IP
- Global facilitator network
- Digital management
- Content creation
- Community distribution engine

### Commercial Models

| Model | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Referral Partner | 10% commission on confirmed venue revenue for introduced leads |
| Programming Partner | Kācima receives venue/food revenue; People Ocean receives 15–25% of net retreat profit |
| Hosted Retreats | 10–20% discount on standard pricing; minimum 2-night booking |
| Strategic Co-branded | Monthly retainer + revenue share; minimum annual retreat commitment |

### Pilot Structure (6 Months)

**Objectives**:
- Position Kācima as retreat-ready venue
- Build retreat information and sales materials
- Launch Kācima within People Ocean network
- Host at least 1 People Ocean-led retreat
- Host or source at least 1 third-party retreat
- Execute August Open-Door Festival
- Generate first set of testimonials

**Success Metrics**:
- Number of qualified facilitator leads
- Number of retreats confirmed
- Revenue generated
- Guest satisfaction
